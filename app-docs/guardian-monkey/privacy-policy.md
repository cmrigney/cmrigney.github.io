# Guardian Monkey Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 14, 2026

## Overview

Guardian Monkey ("the Game") is developed with your privacy in mind. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS game.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Guardian Monkey does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or any other information about you or your device. The Game operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

## Third-Party Services

Guardian Monkey does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or other services that would collect your information.

## Data Storage

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Guardian Monkey does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
