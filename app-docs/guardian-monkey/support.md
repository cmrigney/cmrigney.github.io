# Guardian Monkey - Support

## About

Guardian Monkey is a tower defense game where you control a flying monkey defending a castle from waves of enemies using banana-based attacks.

## How to Play

- Tap and drag to move the monkey
- Collect attack power-ups that drop during waves
- Tap attack slots to switch between collected attacks
- Defend your castle from all incoming enemies to survive each wave
- Earn points for defeating enemies and completing waves

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I unlock new attacks?**
A: Attack power-ups drop randomly during waves. Collect them to add new attacks to your inventory slots.

**Q: How does difficulty scaling work?**
A: The first 9 waves follow set patterns. After wave 9, enemy difficulty scales using a formula that increases enemy count, speed, and variety.

**Q: Can I reset my high scores?**
A: Deleting and reinstalling the app will reset all saved data including high scores and wave progress.

**Q: Is an internet connection required?**
A: No. Guardian Monkey is fully offline and does not require an internet connection.
